Cheletenham Festival -day 1 /

RSA Chase

RACE DETAILS
Betting Odds Race Details Race Tips Recacard/Form Recent Results

The Old Roan Chase is a key Cheltenham Festival Trial which is held right at the start of the season in late October.

The race is run over two miles fourfurlongs at Aintree and maybe we will see some Ryanair Chase contenders for Cheltenham make their seasonal debuts at Aintree for the Old Roan this season. The race is limited handicap race and it has been won by some top class chasers down the years.

The stand out name on the roll of honour is that of the great Kauto Star who won the Old Roan in 2006 before going onto win the Cheltenham Gold cup at the Festival later that same session. Albertas Run won the race in 2011 and he is dual Ryanair Chase winner at the Festival which only serves to highlight the importance of this early season renewal.
